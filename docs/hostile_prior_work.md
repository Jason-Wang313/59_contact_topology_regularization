## Why these are hostile

These papers are the closest because they already touch one or more of:

- contact structure
- tactile graphs
- force control
- contact-safe RL
- topological representations

The proposed paper remains novel only if it regularizes the topology of contact evolution itself, rather than just using graphs, contact states, or force penalties as inputs.
